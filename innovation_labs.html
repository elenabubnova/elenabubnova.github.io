Innovation Labs

My Role: Lead Designer

Innovation Labs BMC subbrand

The Laboratory incubates and accelerates innovative ideas in a startup mode at BMC using small experiments that support an innovation mindset and entrepreneurship unshackled by legacy constraints or immediate operational considerations.

Part of the strategy was to hire top talented engineers and data scientists. The BMC for those people is reliable, but a 40-year-old company. The company is innovative and keeps pace with the times, and the new brand should support that idea.

Solution

The engineers are a specific audience with their own visual code language. The idea was focused on a collaborative space where people can think outside the box.

The logotype is representing brackets from code with a combination of the actual floor plan of that space and an open line that acts as an open door from all limitations.

Web Page

The concept is implemented in dark colors, connecting to the natively integrated development environment for developers. The images are designed as a collage from photography and elements that resemble a constructor or kit in an engineer’s hands.

Social media

The part of the campaign was to create a look and feel for the campaign on social media (primarily LinkedIn) to attract specialists to the company.

Advertising

To catch the attention of professionals, we needed to speak in their language—literally. So we used Python, JavaScript, HTML in this campaign and played in charades. Some messages were kept simple for non-techy stuff.

Signages

When there is a need for graphics on the website or in other communications, here are some examples of directions on how the brand can be developed further.

Interior

The primary goal of the interior space of the Innovation Labs is to create a collaborative space where people can interact and be inspired. Details of the interior should contain brand colors and elements throughout the space. Motivational messages and signages should be included to enhance creativity and innovation.

Brand Guideline

Since a lot of development was handed over to vendors to BMC offices worldwide on-site, it was important to create a Guideline to keep consistency across the company.
