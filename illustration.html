Illustrations

My Role: Lead Designer

Objective

To provide a set of clear and concise guidelines for using illustrations in BMC's marketing, product, and video content, ensuring they complement the brand identity while enhancing storytelling, emotional connection, and visual simplicity.

Solution

Create simple, geometric, and bold illustrations with 3-4 colors that support the messaging and brand style. Use these illustrations sparingly to complement marketing materials, product UI, and videos, ensuring they help explain complex content in a more engaging, easy-to-understand way. Keep the designs clean, modern, and unexpectedly fun while maintaining alignment with BMC's bold and bright brand identity.
