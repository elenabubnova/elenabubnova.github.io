Brand Enhancement

My Role: Lead Designer

The new identity was launched in 2018 and helped bring coherence and consistency to the BMC brand. The company continues to be innovative and has a modern vision for its services and products. It was time to take things to the next level.

Objective

The brand is built on personas, a bright color system, and symbols that help represent a variety of BMC products and services. Part of the brand was a ripple effect that stood out from competitors. However, after two years of use, the marketing team felt that the brand needed a bolder language element in communications.

Concept

The new element “diagonals” was created to represent speed, agility, and efficiency. The personas got new “Bold”, “Curious” and “Unstoppable” characteristics with a visually more emotional and bright style. The typography was also revised to be more bolded.

Placements

Revised branding included all marketing materials: collaterals templates, PowerPoint presentation templates, web banners, social media templates, advertising, infographics, email banners, etc.

Challenge

The new enhancements should harmoniously get along with all other elements and live balanced on all materials.

System

The system is pretty flexible, open to modification, and has a lot of perspectives for development in the future.

Advertisement

For the advertisement campaign, a new concept was created to make BMC stand out from competitors on the internet. As a result, the new brand style became recognizable to potential customers.

Booth graphics

Even though events never happened in 2020, the new brand also influenced the booth graphics. The new guideline was updated with a fresh style and includes modern ideas on how to improve customer experience and attract more people to the stand.
