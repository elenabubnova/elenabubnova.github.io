Hundred Percent Club
Maui 2020

My Role: Lead Designer

Objective

“Hundred percent club” is a recognition program and part of a Sales kick-off event. Every year the most successful sales managers getting a chance to win travel to Hawaii and spend a vacation with family in a Luxury hotel with an entertainment program from BMC.

The goal was to create an event branding with a premium look and feel that will accompany all the way in this travel from the airport until the last sunset.

Concept

This concept was inspired by the Tiki image in Polynesian culture. There are four great gods in Hawaiian mythology:

  • Kane - created light

  • Lono - god of rainfall, fertility, and agriculture, as well as music and peace

  • Ku - god of politics and war

  • Kanaloa - god of the underworld and a teacher of magic

Since the program has existed for about five years in a row, the challenge was not to repeat the previous year's styles.

Placements

The branding includes badges, luggage tags, transportation signages, notecards, letterhead, brochure, pull-up banners, t-shirts, menus, and many others.
