Objective

The new character should be recognizable, customizable, and reflect the brand. It should be marketed over multiple platforms, including the web, social media, and events.

Solution

The graphic style and implementation of the Mascot totally aligned with the brand vision. Fun and powerful look and feel inviting people through engaging emotional part.

The chatbot is really helpful in web and mobile interactions, catching up attention, and drawing it to needed details, became the memorable element and created strong human-like communication with the user.

Web Advertising campaign

The idea of applying the Chatbot to advertising allows to spread the voice and tone of the product, make a visual connection with the message and create the feeling of natural communication.
